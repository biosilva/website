about me

Hello! My name is Fábio Silva and I live in Zurich, Switzerland. I studied Visual Information Technology at the Faculty of Science and Technology at the University of Coimbra and completed a Masters degree in Design & Multimedia.

I received an interdisciplinary education focused on digital media by combining a training in design with elements of information technology.

On my master thesis I developed hi–reader, a native iPad application that provides elaborate reading features with a simple and intuitive user interface. The project combined my strong interests in typography, interaction design and editorial design.

Further projects included brand design, print media, packaging, typeface design, motion graphics, animation, web design and data visualization.
