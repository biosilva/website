edition vfo — 2014

web design — interaction design

The Edition VFO website Verein für Originalgraphik (the Association for Graphic Arts, or VFO) has published graphic works by contemporary artists. Edition VFO promotes not only traditional techniques such as lithography, etching, silk screen or woodcut but also engages in contemporary reproduction methods such as inkjet printing or video animation.

The main focus of this project was simplicity. By using black and white as main colors, the goal was to highlight the artists' work to be the most important content in the website.
