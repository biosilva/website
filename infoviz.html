higher education and employment in portugal — 2011

information visualization — information architecture

The main purpose of this project was to create a dynamic information visualization, where the user could navigate and find information. This infoviz is based on the percentage of graduate students and the employability in Portugal.

It is divided in three topics: university population, containing freshman and graduated students; employed population, concerning graduated employed population; and unemployed population, relative to graduated unemployed population.
